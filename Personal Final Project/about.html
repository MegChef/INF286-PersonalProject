About Me & Us

Hello! My name is Megan. I started crocheting my junior year of high school. The first thing I ever tried crocheting was a fish for my boyfriend. It took me forever and I found it very discouraging. But it really helped me to bring everything back to basics. I watched videos on each stitch and watched someone else follow a written pattern. This really helped me, so I wanted to make this website to be a space where you can find the basics and try them out on easy crochet patterns. I also wanted to make it interactive so that we can show our crochet journeys together.
